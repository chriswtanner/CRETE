Over 300 Russian police search for assailants after attack on
tourists
MOSCOW, June 13 (Xinhua)
Over 300 police officers were fanning out to track down the assailants who
attacked Moscow tourists in the Russian city of Nizhny Novgorod, RIA Novosti
news agency reported Sunday.
Two armed men with sawn-off guns attacked a group of Moscow tourists late
Saturday, injuring three of them.
The assailants fled the scene in a car, opened fire at police officers who were
trying to stop them, and then disappeared in a nearby forest.
Two officers were injured and in grave condition, according to doctors.
